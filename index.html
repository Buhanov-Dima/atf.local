Home appliances

Series for home cultivation of edible plants. Allows to grow tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers, strawberries, salad and other plants autonomously. Series for experienced growers (cannabis can be also grown).

Choose the mode – our smart system will do the rest. The series allows you to model all the parameters of growth, it can be used as training equipment in schools.

Industrial equpment

For small businesses and small farms – a series allowing to expand the year-round production shortly, even without special experience. It can also be used in remote military units, working settlements and long-distance expeditions. Put equipment in the cellars of houses and grow fresh products every day.
ATF Industrial is perfect for large investors and agribusiness. Including all the potential and best practices of modern crop production, it will ensure the deployment of large-scale production with a yield of 1 tons per years. ATF farms easily scales to any size of production. This meet the needs of the whole region, even in the far North.
